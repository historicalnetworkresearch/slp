## HNR2027

The 12th International Historical Network Research Conference (HNR2027) will take place on **July 14-16, 2027** at the Universidad Autónoma de San Luis Potosí in San Luis Potosí, Mexico. It brings together researchers applying network research methods across all the historical sciences, and strengthening an international community that grows, connects, and reaches an ever-wider audience.
